title: Inspiring People to Change tags: leadership, ted, people I've always been curious, on a subconscious level, what makes people do what they do. Well, I don't have an answer, but I'm learning about how to change what people do. According to Hildy Gottlieb, [people change](http://hildygottlieb.com/2011/09/21/getting-people-to-change/) when they're inspired to. Simon Sinek has something similar to say in his [TED](http://ted.com) talk. (He has [another talk](http://the99percent.com/videos/7058/Simon-Sinek-If-You-Dont-Understand-People-You-Dont-Understand-Business) that looks promising, but I haven't watched it yet.)
