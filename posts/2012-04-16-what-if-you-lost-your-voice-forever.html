title: What if You Lost Your Voice Forever? tags: ted, communication, society Let's say you've lost your voice, due to some unfortunate happenstance, but instead of it being gone for a couple days or weeks, it's gone forever. You will never again utter a word. Take a moment, pause, and think about how dramatically different your life would be, assuming you're one of the lucky people who can speak. How would you communicate? Would you carry a laptop to say phrases outloud for you? How would that change your lifestyle? This isn't a subject that suddenly occurred to me out of boredom or idleness, I watched a talk given by/about [Roger Ebert](http://rogerebert.suntimes.com/) who has lost his jaw and ability to speak.
